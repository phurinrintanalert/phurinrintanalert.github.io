About

I spent two years as a doctor in the NHS. On the wards, I saw firsthand how systemic bottlenecks and diagnostic delays cost both time and lives — and how often the missing piece wasn’t medical knowledge, but better tools.

So I decided to learn to build them. I’m now completing an MSc in Healthcare Technologies at King’s College London, focusing on computer vision, generative AI, and deep learning applied to medical imaging.

My clinical background shapes everything I build: models that are not just technically rigorous, but clinically relevant and practically deployable. You can find my latest projects on GitHub.

Vision Models for Guidewire Tracking in Fluoroscopy 2026 · MSc Thesis

The perception component of a world model for autonomous endovascular navigation. I systematically benchmarked ten architectures for markerless guidewire tracking on 10,000 simulated fluoroscopy frames, and found that self-supervised foundation-model features (DINOv3) dominate — a DINOv3+CNN adapter cut tracking error 1.8× over a raw-pixel CNN, while temporal memory significantly bounded worst-case error under acute image corruption.

  • PyTorch
  • GCN / LSTM

Age-Conditioned Neonatal Brain MRI Synthesis 2026

Pediatric neuroimaging suffers from severe data scarcity. I built an end-to-end PyTorch pipeline that synthesizes gestational-age-conditioned neonatal brain MRIs with latent diffusion — a custom age-conditioned U-Net and a VAE optimized to eliminate upscaling artifacts — then validated image quality with an auxiliary ResNet performing brain-age estimation on the synthetic scans.

Denoising Photoacoustic Imaging Data 2026

A comparative study of denoising strategies for photoacoustic RF data acquired on a real ultrasound system. Beyond benchmarking wavelet, VMD, spatiotemporal SVD, Robust PCA and Kalman methods, I uncovered a data-leakage flaw that had inflated the accepted evaluation metric, and introduced two new baseline-relative metrics (Signal Preservation and Background Energy) that expose algorithms which "cheat" by suppressing the whole image rather than genuinely denoising it.

  • MATLAB
  • Python
  • Signal Processing
  • SVD / VMD
  • Photoacoustic Imaging
